About

I am pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering at the University of Massachusetts Amherst, with a strong academic foundation and hands-on experience in software development, hardware prototyping, and electrical systems.

Through my course work I have honed my technical expertise and problem-solving abilities. My hands-on projects include writing drivers for a BNO055, coding a linear regression model to predict housing market trends, and designing interactive educational games using JavaScript.

Beyond the classroom, I’ve gained practical experience working with student organizations, where I collaborated closely with peers. Recently, I've been working with the UMASS CubeSat Team to research and create a BMS and Power Distribution System, as well as researching PCIe to USB data transmission.

I enjoy exploring new advancements in technology, experimenting with innovative ideas, and learning about emerging trends in computer engineering. I am excited to connect with like-minded professionals and contribute to impactful projects that push the boundaries of innovation.
