About Me

I am pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering at the University of Massachusetts Amherst, with a strong academic foundation and hands-on experience in software development, hardware prototyping, and electrical systems.

Through my coursework I have honed my technical expertise and problem-solving abilities. My projects include writing drivers for a BNO055, coding a linear regression model to predict housing market trends, and designing interactive educational games using JavaScript.

Beyond the classroom, I’ve gained practical experience working with student organizations, collaborating with peers to deliver reliable results. Recently, I've been contributing to the UMASS CubeSat Team to research and create a BMS and Power Distribution System, as well as investigating PCIe to USB data transmission.

I enjoy exploring new advancements in technology, experimenting with innovative ideas, and learning about emerging trends in computer engineering. I am excited to connect with like-minded professionals and contribute to projects that push the boundaries of innovation.
